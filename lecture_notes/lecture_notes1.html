Intro to Torch

AATorch is quite popular in top research labs around the world, like Facebook and Google (heavily used in Google DeepMind). It started from \emph{Y. LeCun's} lab, who is one of the leading experts in deep learning. The underlying language, Lua, resembles the Matlab coding style, namely it is a scripting language. Torch and Lua are rich in features and relatively easy to use (although annoying some times) and, therefore, are ideal for rapid prototyping.
